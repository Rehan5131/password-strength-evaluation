STRONG PASSWORD CREATION TIPS:
1. Use 12–16+ characters.
2. Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special symbols.
3. Avoid dictionary words, names, and predictable sequences.
4. Use creative substitutions like E=3, A=@, O=0.
5. Don’t use personal information like birthdays or names.
6. Prefer passphrases with unrelated words (e.g., Tree#Laptop9*River).
7. Use a password manager for unique credentials on each account.
8. Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) wherever possible.
9. Regularly update passwords for sensitive accounts.
10. Be aware of phishing and never share your passwords.
